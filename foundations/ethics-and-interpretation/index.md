## Understanding AI Ethics in Visualization

This section explores the ethical considerations when developing visualization tools enhanced with artificial intelligence.

### Key Topics

- **Bias in Visualization** - How biases can be introduced and how to mitigate them
- **Data Privacy** - Protecting sensitive information in visual representations
- **Interpretability** - Making AI decisions transparent through visualization
- **Attribution** - Proper credit for data sources and AI models
- **Accessibility** - Ensuring visualizations work for all users

### Why It Matters

Visualization is a powerful communication tool. When combined with AI, it can amplify both insights and misinformation. Understanding the ethical implications helps create responsible solutions.
